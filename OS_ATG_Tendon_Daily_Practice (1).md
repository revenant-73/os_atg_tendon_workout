# OS – ATG – Tendon Daily Practice (Big Lift Integrated)

**Prepared, Not Tired**  
8–10 Weeks • 3 Days/Week (+ Optional 4th)  
Same workout every session. Progression happens across weeks, not days.

---

## DAILY SESSION STRUCTURE (Always the Same)

### 1. RESET (5–7 minutes)

1. Supine breathing – 5–8 slow breaths  
2. Crocodile breathing – 5–8 slow breaths  
3. Neck nods (yes/no) – 10–15 reps  
4. Rocking (quadruped or seated) – 15–20 reps  
5. Rolling (supine ↔ prone) – 4–6 total  
6. Easy crawl (optional) – 30–60 seconds  

---

### 2. TENDON ISOMETRICS (6–8 minutes)

7. Bent-knee calf isometric  
8. Tibialis raises (reps or isometric)  
9. Split squat isometric hold  

*Block-based duration:*  
- Weeks 1–2: 20–30s  
- Weeks 3–4: 30–45s  
- Weeks 5–6: 45–60s  
- Weeks 7–10: 30–45s (maintenance)

---

### 3. ATG STRENGTH (8–12 minutes)

10. ATG split squat (controlled reps + pauses)  
11. Hip flexor breathing (half-kneeling or supine) – 5–8 breaths/side  
12. Wall sit (optional)  
13. Deep squat breathing (optional)  
14. Light hinge pattern (optional)  

*Choose only one optional movement (12–14).*

---

### 4. BIG LIFT SLOT (Optional, 5–10 minutes)

**Only 2–3 sessions per week. Never mandatory.**  
**Always after ATG strength, before integration.**

15. Sled push (default)  
16. Sled pull (default alternative)  

**Optional alternates (later blocks only):**  
17. Heavy kettlebell swings  
18. Jefferson curls  

*Rules:*  
- One big lift per session  
- Stop early  
- Calm breathing within 60–90 seconds  

---

### 5. INTEGRATION (3–6 minutes)

19. Forward crawl  
20. Backward crawl  
21. Lateral crawl  
22. Bear hold  
23. Marching (slow, controlled)  
24. Sit → stand → floor transitions  

*Choose 1–2 movements.*

---

### 6. OPTIONAL EXPLORATION (10–15 minutes)

25. Longer crawling session  
26. Ground flow  
27. Carries  
28. Very light sled work  
29. Get-up patterns  
30. Mobility exploration  

*Skip anytime without penalty.*

---

## BLOCK PROGRESSION OVERVIEW

### Block 1 – Restore & Map (Weeks 1–2)
- Big lift: OFF  
- Focus: safety, breathing, coordination  

### Block 2 – Own the Range (Weeks 3–4)
- Big lift: sled only  
- Low volume, nasal breathing  

### Block 3 – Capacity Without Chaos (Weeks 5–6)
- Big lift: sled (primary), KB swings optional  
- 1–2 swing days/week max  

### Block 4 – Integration & Expression (Weeks 7–10)
- Big lift: sled, KB swings, or Jefferson curls  
- Intuitive rotation  

---

## DAILY READINESS GUIDE

- **Green:** Full session  
- **Yellow:** ~50% volume, shorter holds  
- **Red:** Reset only  

---

## KEY PRINCIPLES

- Same workout every day  
- Missed days require no adjustment  
- Finish calmer than you started  
- Strength supports the system, it does not lead it
